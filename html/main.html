Class Motivations

Extrinsic Motivation

Receiving financial rewards motivates me to perform better.

Achieving high grades motivates me to put more effort into my work.

Being publicly recognized for my work motivates me to perform better.

Receiving praise from instructors or supervisors motivates me to improve my performance.

Intrinsic Motivation

My curiosity about the subject motivates me to put more effort into my coursework.

The desire to improve my skills and develop mastery motivates me to perform better in my coursework.

Enjoying the process of learning and solving problems motivates me to perform better in my coursework.

Believing that what I am learning is meaningful or valuable motivates me to put more effort into my coursework.
